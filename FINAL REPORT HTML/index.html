The idea
 
 
DeliverIt is a smartphone application that helps businesses send deliveries using simple touch. Home-delivery requests are sent to agents who are willing to offer the service on a pay-per-delivery basis.
 
 

Listening to
your needs

DeliverIT aims to ease the process of delivery management for midsized businesses.

Agent
discovery

We make use of location services to find nearby agents(drivers) who will make deliveries for customers through a simple platform.

Cost
effective

This innovative application addresses staffing issues, cuts down transportation cost and provides a means of income for agents who opt for delivery.

Getting
there

We use an automated driver-routing algorithm for taking the smartest route between restaurants and a customer’s home. .

Delivering
the order

Besides providing a fast and efficient delivery, we use a unique security mechanism where deliveries can be confirmed through order delivery codes sent to customers.

 
The implementation
 
 
Key Activities
 

Selecting
driver

Once the restaurant receives an order, the restaurant will broadcast a request with the destination of the order to all the cab drivers currently operating. The drivers then will have the option of accepting or declining the order from their end.

Food
delivery

Once the driver opts to deliver the order, the details including the destination of the order, the route and the estimated time to reach will be shown on his application.

Our motivation
 
 
Our research shows that restaurants spend a lot of time and money in delivering food to their customers whom they value the most. The owners have to employ more number of resources and bear transportation costs only to cater to the high frequency of home deliveries. Often, small local restaurants fail to have a food delivery service owing to the lack of funding. This hurts customers too who cannot avail such options from their favorite local restaurants.

Hence, DeliverIt comes to the rescue of the restaurants with a cost effective and efficient solution. This application addresses the spending issues of the restaurant owners with a low cost business model. It focuses on low-end market disruption.
